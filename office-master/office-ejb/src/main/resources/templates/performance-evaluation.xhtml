Instructions:

This is an annual performance evaluation. Managers with direct reports must electronically complete, print and sign the form and provide it to HR for compliance review/approval. After the review process has been completed managers should provide each employee with a copy of his/her review and forward all original documents to the Human Resources department to be immediately secured in the employee’s file.
