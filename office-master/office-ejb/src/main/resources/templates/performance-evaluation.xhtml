PERFORMANCE EVALUATION

Instructions:

This is an annual performance evaluation. Managers with direct reports must electronically complete, print and sign the form and provide it to HR for compliance review/approval. Afterwards, managers should review performance evaluations with each employee. Employees may add any relevant comments and sign, acknowledging review and receipt of their evaluation. After the review process has been completed managers should provide each employee with a copy of his/her review and forward all original documents to the Human Resources department to be immediately secured in the employee’s file.
