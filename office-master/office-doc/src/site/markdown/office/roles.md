How to change roles for various users
-----
 - [Login to Portal](../office/forgot-password.html "Login")
 - Search for an employee by navigating through **My Office** -> **Employees**.
 - Either click on **view** or **update** icons to add/remove a role.
 - Navigate through **Roles** to **Add** or **Remove** roles.
 - Select a role and click on **Add** button so that role will be added.
 - For removing, select a role and click on **Remove**, it will be removed.
