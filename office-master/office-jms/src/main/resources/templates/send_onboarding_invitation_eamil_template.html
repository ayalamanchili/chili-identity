2. I-9 Verification Supporting Documents: as requested in the I-9 verification form, all employees must also submit documentation supporting their eligibility to work in the US. Please send copies of all identity/work authorization documents you choose to list on your I-9. Depending on your status, commonly used documents include:

- H-1: copies of the first and last pages of your passport and your current I-94.

- EAD: copies of the front and back of your EAD card.

- US CITIZEN: a valid copy of the first page of your passport; OR a photo ID, such as your driver’s license along with your social security card.

If you are OPT/CPT, please send a copy of your current I-20. We will then provide you a letter to submit to your school in order to add as your employer for your new I-20.

3. W4: this form determines the number of deductions you elect to be withdrawn from your monthly paychecks. Line 5 must be filled in and marital status checked in order for us to process this form. Please do not choose "married but withhold at higher single rate;" "single" and "married" are the only electable options in ADP. If you'd like additional monies withheld, please select "married" and indicate on line 6 the specific dollar amount you would like to be withheld.
