Please review the example I-9 form before filling out the I-9 form. If you have any questions about completing this document accurately, please contact me at 727 723 0801 x312.

 

The I-9 form is a very important document... SST requests that you either use the fields to populate the information or use a black pen when completing and/or signing it. We do require the ORIGINAL signed document for your employee file. Please mail it to us after signing and dating. The date you sign it MUST BE on or before your start date with SST.

 

The W4 form must have line 5 filled in and marital status checked! Please do not choose "married but withhold at higher single rate" ... To process our payroll, which is set up through ADP, we only have "single" or "married" as choices. If you'd like additional monies withheld, please choose "married" then indicate on line 6 a specific dollar amount to be withheld.

Make sure that all forms are signed, dated and returned ASAP... joining forms must be completed for you to be added into our payroll. Also, your joining date cannot be prior to the date that you submit the completed forms to us.

 

Please send copies of all identity/work authorization documents you choose to list on your I-9. Depending on your status, commonly used documents include

H-1-

copies of the first and last pages of your passport and your current I-94

EAD -

copies of the front and back of that card.

US Citizen -

a valid copy of the first page of your passport; OR a Photo ID, such as your Driver's license along with SSN card.

Also, if you are OPT/CPT, please send a copy of your current I-20; we will provide you a letter for your school to add System Soft Technologies as your employer for your new I-20.
